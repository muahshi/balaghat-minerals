Manganese Dioxide (MnO₂ 70-85%): USD 300–450/MT

Prices vary by grade, quantity, and Incoterms. Submit an enquiry for a firm quote within 4 hours.

Products & Grades
We supply three primary products:

1. Manganese Ore — 30–44% Mn, available as lumps (25–80mm) or fines (0–10mm).
2. Manganese Oxide (MnO) — 20–55% MnO, powder and granular form for industrial, feed and fertilizer applications.
3. Manganese Dioxide (MnO₂) — 30–80% MnO₂, granular form for battery, chemical and water treatment industries.
Manganese Ore: Steel manufacturing (ferro-manganese, silico-manganese alloys), welding electrode production, glass manufacturing.

Manganese Oxide: Animal feed supplement (Mn micronutrient), fertilizers, ceramics and glazes, chemical industry.

Manganese Dioxide: Dry cell batteries, water purification (filtration media), specialty chemicals, oxidizing agent in chemical synthesis.

Export & Shipping
We export worldwide. Key markets include Vietnam, Indonesia, Malaysia, South Korea, Japan (South East Asia), UAE, Saudi Arabia, Oman, Egypt (Middle East & Africa), Germany, Netherlands, Turkey, Belgium (Europe), and the USA, Brazil (Americas). We ship to any country with no trade restrictions with India.
We ship from Mundra Port (Gujarat) and JNPT / Nhava Sheva (Mumbai). Both FCL (Full Container Load) and LCL (Less than Container Load) are available. We typically use 20ft and 40ft containers. Road transport to port is included in FOB/CIF pricing.

Payment Terms
TT (Telegraphic Transfer / Wire Transfer): 30% advance with order, 70% against copy of Bill of Lading.

LC (Letter of Credit) at sight: Irrevocable LC from a reputed bank accepted for orders above 50 MT.

We do not accept personal cheques. Bank details provided on Proforma Invoice.
We invoice in USD (US Dollars) for all export orders. EUR invoicing is available on request for European buyers. Domestic orders are invoiced in INR.
Quality & Documentation
Yes, every shipment includes a Certificate of Analysis (COA) from our in-house lab reporting: Mn%, Fe%, SiO₂, Al₂O₃, CaO, MgO, moisture content, and mesh/particle size analysis. We can also arrange third-party inspection by SGS or Bureau Veritas at the buyer's cost.
