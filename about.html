Our Story

Built on the
Ore We Know

M/s Balaghat Minerals was founded in the heart of Madhya Pradesh's manganese belt — Balaghat district, home to some of India's richest and most consistent manganese ore deposits.

We built our own processing plant to control every step from raw ore to finished product: crushing, screening, grading, lab testing, and packaging. No outsourcing. No middlemen in the supply chain. Just direct, traceable, reliable manganese at manufacturer prices.

"Every lot we ship carries a Certificate of Analysis. We don't hide behind grade ranges — we tell you exactly what you're getting, down to the decimal."

Today we serve steel mills, chemical manufacturers, battery producers, and commodity traders across Asia, the Middle East, Europe, and the Americas — all from the same facility in Balaghat.

Strategic Advantage

Why Balaghat is
India's Manganese Hub

  • Balaghat district sits atop one of India's largest manganese ore deposits — the same belt mined by MOIL (Manganese Ore India Limited), a Government of India enterprise.
  • India is among the world's top 5 manganese producers. Balaghat ore is known for consistent grade and low impurity levels compared to other Indian sources.
  • Proximity to ore source means lower input costs, faster replenishment, and better grade control for our buyers.
  • Strategic location between Nagpur and Jabalpur — well-connected by road and rail for efficient transport to major Indian ports.
  • In-house lab testing infrastructure built specifically for manganese analysis: Mn%, Fe%, SiO₂, Al₂O₃, moisture, mesh size.
