Applications

Industrial Uses of
Manganese Oxide

Animal Feed
MnO is an essential micronutrient supplement in poultry, cattle and swine feed. Approved for use in animal nutrition globally.
Fertilizers
Used as manganese micronutrient in agricultural fertilizers to correct Mn-deficient soils, improving crop yield.
Ceramics & Glazes
Colorant in ceramic glazes and enamels, producing brown to black shades. Used in tile, pottery and dinnerware manufacturing.
Chemical Industry
Intermediate for production of other manganese compounds including MnSO4 (manganese sulfate) and catalysts.
Welding Flux
Used in submerged arc welding flux formulations to control slag chemistry and improve weld mechanical properties.
Pigments & Paints
MnO used as a drying agent (drier) in linseed oil paints and varnishes. Also used as a brown-black pigment.
