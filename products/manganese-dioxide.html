Applications

Industrial Uses of
Manganese Dioxide

Battery Manufacturing
Key cathode material in zinc-carbon and alkaline dry cell batteries. Also used in lithium-MnO₂ primary batteries.
Water Treatment
Used as filter media to remove iron, manganese, hydrogen sulfide and arsenic from drinking water and industrial wastewater.
Chemical Synthesis
Strong oxidizing agent in organic chemical synthesis. Used in production of hydroquinone, benzaldehyde and other intermediates.
Glass & Ceramics
Decolorant in glassmaking and colorant producing amber to black hues in glass and ceramic glazes.
Electronics
Used in manufacture of ferrites for electronic components, soft magnetic cores and microwave absorbers.
Specialty Chemicals
Feedstock for production of potassium permanganate (KMnO₄) and other high-value manganese compounds.
