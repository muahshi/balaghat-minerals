Company Overview

Real Manufacturers.
No Middlemen.

M/s Balaghat Minerals operates its own processing plant in Balaghat, Madhya Pradesh — the heart of India's most productive manganese ore belt. We source directly from mine owners, process in-house, and supply finished products to buyers across India and 15+ export countries.

Our buyer base spans domestic steel mills, ferro-alloy plants, battery manufacturers, fertilizer companies, and chemical industries — all supplied under consistent specifications with Certificate of Analysis on every lot. No brokers. No inflated margins. Direct factory pricing.

Quality Assurance

Every Lot.
Lab Tested. Documented.

In-House Lab Testing
Every lot tested for Mn%, Fe%, SiO2, Phosphorus, moisture and particle size before dispatch. Results documented in COA.
Certificate of Analysis
Detailed COA issued with every shipment. Accepted by domestic mills and international buyers as primary quality document.
Third-Party Inspection
SGS, Bureau Veritas, or buyer-nominated inspection arranged pre-shipment on request at buyer's cost.
Weighbridge Certificate
Accurate dispatch weight documented at our weighbridge. Supplied with every delivery for transparent payment reconciliation.
Consistent Packaging
HDPE woven bags (25kg/50kg) or bulk jumbo bags. Labelled with grade, lot number, weight. Custom packaging available.
Industries Served

Eight Industries.
One Reliable Source.

Steel Mills
Manganese ore as alloying agent in steel production for strength and hardness
Ferro-Alloys
Ore input for ferro-manganese and silico-manganese production in electric arc furnaces
Battery Mfg
MnO₂ as cathode material in alkaline and zinc-carbon batteries
Fertilizers
MnO as micronutrient in compound fertilizers for manganese-deficient soil correction
Animal Feed
MnO as essential trace mineral supplement in livestock and poultry feed formulations
Water Treatment
MnO₂ filtration media for iron, manganese and H₂S removal from municipal and industrial water
Ceramics & Glass
MnO as colorant and flux in ceramic glazes and decolorising agent in glass manufacturing
Chemicals
MnO₂ as oxidising agent and precursor in specialty chemical and pharmaceutical synthesis

Logistics & Supply Chain

Road. Rail. Sea.
Pan India & Export.

Dispatching to domestic buyers across India and exporting internationally through India's major bulk ports — on your timeline, on your Incoterms.

Truck-based door delivery to buyers across MP, Maharashtra, West Bengal, Gujarat and other steel/ferro-alloy hubs. Weighbridge certificate included.
