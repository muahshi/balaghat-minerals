Company Overview

Who We Are

M/s Balaghat Minerals is a direct manufacturer and global bulk exporter of manganese products, operating from Balaghat, Madhya Pradesh — located at the centre of one of India's most productive manganese ore belts.

We own and operate our processing plant, giving us full control over quality, consistency, and pricing at every stage from raw ore procurement to finished product dispatch. There are no brokers, no trading margins, and no compromises on material quality.

Our buyer base spans domestic steel mills and ferro-alloy plants across India, as well as international customers in Southeast Asia, the Middle East, and beyond — all supplied under consistent specifications with Certificate of Analysis on every lot.

Mission

To be the most reliable manganese supply partner in India — delivering consistent quality, transparent pricing, and on-time dispatch every time.

Vision

To expand our processing capacity and export footprint, becoming the preferred direct-source manganese supplier for industrial buyers worldwide.

Values

Consistency over shortcuts. Quality that doesn't vary batch to batch. Long-term partnerships over transactional deals.

Location Advantage

Why Balaghat?

Balaghat district in Madhya Pradesh sits on one of India's most significant manganese ore deposits. Our location at the source of ore supply means shorter procurement cycles, lower raw material cost, and tighter control over incoming ore quality before processing begins.

  • Balaghat district is among India's highest manganese ore-producing regions, with decades of established mining and processing infrastructure.
  • Proximity to ore sources reduces logistics costs and allows faster turnaround on custom grade procurement vs. buyers sourcing from distant markets.
  • Well-connected to Mundra Port (Gujarat) and JNPT (Mumbai) — India's primary bulk mineral export terminals — for FCL and LCL shipments.
  • Pan-India road and rail connectivity enables domestic supply to steel mills and ferro-alloy plants across Maharashtra, West Bengal, Gujarat, and beyond.
  • GST registered (23AAUFB1933D1ZP) · IEC registered · Verified supplier on major trade platforms.

Quality Assurance

Every Lot.
Lab Tested.

We don't ship what we haven't tested. Every production lot is sampled and tested before dispatch, with Certificate of Analysis provided to the buyer covering all critical parameters.

  • In-House Lab Testing

    Every lot tested for Mn%, Fe%, SiO2, Phosphorus, moisture and particle size before dispatch.

  • Certificate of Analysis (COA)

    Detailed COA issued with every shipment — accepted by domestic mills and international buyers as the primary quality document.

  • Third-Party Inspection Available

    SGS, Bureau Veritas, or buyer-nominated inspection can be arranged pre-shipment on request.

  • Weighbridge Certificate

    Accurate dispatch weight documented at our weighbridge. Supplied with every delivery for payment reconciliation.

  • Consistent Packaging

    HDPE woven bags (25kg / 50kg) or bulk jumbo bags. Clearly labelled with grade, lot number, and weight. Custom packaging available.
